What are Mental Health Problems?

Mental health problems encompass a vast spectrum of conditions that profoundly influence an individuals emotional, psychological, and social well-being, thereby affecting how they think, feel, and interact with others on a daily basis. These conditions manifest in diverse forms, ranging from anxiety disorders-where individuals might experience ongoing, uncontrollable worry and fear-to mood disorders, such as depression, characterized by persistent feelings of sadness, hopelessness, and a diminished interest in activities that once brought fulfillment and joy. It is important for us to show recognition and to show acts of altruism around these people.
